Rock concerts in Milan

This summer Italian rock fans were treated to a stunning performance by DjFunk. Milan concert made a lasting impression on the audience making it a concert to remember for months to come. This, along with a completely sold out show, caused quite a stir- the crowd was excitable and energized, all ready to rock for the night! This is an ambitious musical project that is sure to make a comeback on summer 2015.
